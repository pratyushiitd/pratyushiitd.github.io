About Me

Hello! I'm Pratyush Saini, an AI Engineer with a burning passion for pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence. My journey in the world of AI began at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, where I honed my skills in Computer Science and Engineering, laying the foundation for my future innovations.

My professional journey led me to ThirdAI Corp, where I've been at the forefront of developing cutting-edge RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Systems using ThirdAI's NeuralDB. This experience has allowed me to dive deep into the fascinating world of machine learning and natural language processing, creating solutions that bridge the gap between AI and real-world applications.

What drives me is the potential of AI to transform industries and improve lives. Whether it's enhancing decision-making processes, automating complex tasks, or uncovering insights from vast amounts of data, I'm always excited to tackle new challenges and push the boundaries of what's possible with AI.
